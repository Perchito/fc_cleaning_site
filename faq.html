Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

These answers cover the questions commercial clients ask most often before requesting a quote or arranging a site visit.

Do you offer commercial cleaning contracts?

Yes. We provide ongoing commercial cleaning contracts tailored to your site, layout, cleaning frequency, and business hours. You can choose daily, weekly, or custom plans that fit your schedule.

What areas do you cover?

We focus on Manchester and the surrounding areas, with a strong emphasis on offices, shared workplaces, and managed commercial buildings. If you are in or near Manchester, we can usually arrange a site visit and proposal.

Can you clean outside normal business hours?

Yes. We offer flexible scheduling, including evenings, early mornings, and weekends, so cleaning can happen without disrupting your team, visitors, or clients.

Do you provide deep cleaning or one-off cleans?

Yes. We provide deep cleaning and one-off commercial cleans for workplaces that need a reset, a presentation refresh, or extra support before inspections, events, or handovers.

Who do you typically work with?

We work with office managers, facilities teams, property managers, and businesses that need dependable commercial cleaning, clear communication, and consistent presentation standards.

How do you make sure standards stay consistent?

Our approach is shaped by years of hospitality experience, brand audits, and inspection-led standards. We focus on clear checklists, dependable routines, and owner-managed accountability so service stays consistent.

How do we get a quote?

The process is simple. Tell us about your premises and cleaning needs, and we can arrange a quick conversation or a free site visit before sending a tailored proposal.

Do you work with offices and shared commercial spaces only?

Our service is designed primarily for offices, shared workplaces, communal areas, and managed commercial properties. We focus on business environments that need reliable, repeatable cleaning standards.

Our story

Hospitality standards applied to commercial cleaning

Before starting FC Cleaning Company, I spent over 12 years working in hospitality, with 9 of those years managing busy branded stores across the UK. That meant living inside strict brand standards, audits, and independent inspections, and building teams that reliably delivered strong results.

In one year, my teams consistently averaged around 97% across audits, which taught me how powerful good systems, clear checklists, and owner-level oversight can be. Today, I use that same discipline and attention to detail in commercial cleaning, applying hospitality-style standards to offices and shared workplaces in Manchester.

What that means for clients

We do not treat cleaning like a generic box-ticking service. We approach it with structured standards, consistent expectations, and the kind of attention to detail that protects presentation, supports staff, and helps your site stay ready for visitors, audits, and day-to-day business.

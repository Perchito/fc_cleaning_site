Our story

Hospitality standards applied to commercial cleaning

Before starting FC Cleaning Company, I spent more than 12 years in hospitality, including 9 years managing busy branded stores across the UK. That meant working inside strict brand standards, audits, inspections, and operational routines where consistency mattered every day.

In one year, my teams averaged around 97% across audits. That experience shaped how I think about standards: strong results come from clear systems, checklists, accountability, and attention to detail. FC Cleaning applies that same discipline to commercial spaces in Manchester.
